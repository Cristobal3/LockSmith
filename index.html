Our Services

Craig’s Lock & Safe is an award-winning, family-owned and operated locksmith business that stands on integrity. We offer the installation, service, and repair of high security locks, standard locks, safes, and other security equipment and services. We service Sugar Land, Richmond, Rosenberg, Stafford, Katy and Missouri City, TX.

Our Committment

Because we are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, you always have a reliable locksmith that is only a phone call away. Do you need your locks rekeyed? Do you have an emergency lockout situation and need your house or business opened? Have you lost all your keys to a lock that needs to be opened right away? With our mobile locksmith service, we come right to your location to help you with your situation.

Our Values

We’re proud of our family’s decades of experience in the locksmith service industry. We offer highly affordable prices and a name you can trust. Our emphasis on family-values, integrity, quality and customer satisfaction means you are always just one call away from award-winning, expert locksmith professionals.
